Key Features

Verified Listings

We add a verified badge to the listing that underwent our verification process to ensure they are accurate and trustworthy, so you can browse with confidence.

Landlord Verification

All landlords willing to add more than one listing are required to undergo our rigorous verification process to ensure that they are reliable and credible for a safe renting experience.

Financial Health Check

We allow potential tenants to go thorough a financial health check to be able to see the listings that fit their budget and for landlords and to see tenant match level.

House Sharing Listings

Our platform allows landlords to enable sharing on their listings, which allows tenants to form tenant groups and apply to rent the space as roommates or housemates.

Easy Document Preparation

Our platform allows landlords and tenants to easily prepare the various contracts and e-sign them. Easily prepare rental, co-tenant, sublease, and other agreements.
